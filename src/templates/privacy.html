Privacy

Page hits stay lightweight, transparent, and easy to disable.

dout.dev stores page-hit counts in your own browser by default. No cookies, no cross-site identifiers, and no profiling. If a server endpoint is configured later, the client still respects Do Not Track and your local opt-out.

What is recorded

Only page paths and visit counts.

  • Local storage keeps a simple per-page counter in this browser.
  • Do Not Track and Global Privacy Control disable tracking automatically when supported.
  • You can opt out or clear the stored counts at any time from this page.
